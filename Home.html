CONTACT MANAGER

Editing and Ubdating a Contact


A contact manager is a software program that enables users to easily store and find contact information, such as names, addresses and telephone numbers. They are contact-centric databases that provide a fully integrated approach to tracking of all information and communication activities linked to contacts.

BENIFITS OF USING CONTACT MANAGER:

1) Centralized repository of contact information
2) Ready to use database with searching
3) Sales tracking
4) Scheduling of appointments and meetings
5) Managemant of Customizable fields
6) Import/export utility
7) Managing Contacts of electrician, plumber, tailor,etc...
